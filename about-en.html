ABOUT US

TECHNO STAT GROUP | THE TEAM

Who are we?

Techno Stat Group is a Beninese firm created in 2018, specialized in training, research, studies, digital solutions for development, as well as in the construction sector in Africa.

The firm is registered under number RCCM RB/DGOU/2018.A-007 and IFU 0201810225408. Techno Stat is also registered with the tax administration and is up to date with its tax obligations as well as its social contributions (CNSS).

Vision

To be a reference structure in research, digital innovation, and infrastructure development, contributing to sustainable and inclusive development in Africa.

Mission

To provide quality services in research, studies and impact assessment, training, and digital solutions, as well as in infrastructure engineering, to improve the living conditions of communities and promote sustainable development. We combine skills in socio-economic studies, technology, and construction expertise to offer solutions adapted to local needs.
